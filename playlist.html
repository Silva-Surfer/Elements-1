The Infinite Wrench Goes Viral

ELEMENTS OF STYLE Elements of Style is a Neo-Futurist musical about communication in response to William Strunk Jr. and E.B. White’s classic grammar textbook “The Elements of Style.” Led by Ensemble Member Trent Lunsford, this show examines the many ways language and words shape our identity, performed in English and American Sign Language. // SUMMER 2023 // //CREATED BY TRENT LUNSFORD AND MARY KATE ASHE //
